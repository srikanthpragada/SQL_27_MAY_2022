Books Catalog
=============
Publishers  - id, name, email, phone 
Topics - id, name
Authors - id, name, email, phone 
Titles  - isbn, title, publisher, topic, release date, cover type, price in INR
TitleAuthors  - isbn, author 


Notes:
------
1. Each table must have a primary key
2. Cover type of a title is either H - hard cover or P - Paper back
3. Price of book is an integer
4. All ids and isbn are integers and autoincrement 
5. A single title may have many authors
6. Email address must be unique in each table
